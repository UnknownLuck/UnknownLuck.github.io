UnknownLuck

Maker. Programmer. FPV Pilot.

Jacob Ursua

I have always had a huge interest in technology ever since I was a young child. Growing up, I've had many opportunities to learn and discover new cool things about the industry that can be found all around us, technology of course!

Playing video games was a big hobby in my childhood, and eventually lead to my interest in learning how to create video games. From that, I started learning to program, teaching me even more about computers. Currently I do web development and some minor game development.

When I found 3D printing, I was instantly hooked! Being able to create nearly anything one can think of is incredible. I find many models online, as well as designing lots of my own items for many of my personal projects.

But if I had to talk about what my favorite hobby is, I would have to respond with flying FPV drones. FPV, (or first person view), is a hobby in which a drone with a camera is used to fly fast and to do crazy tricks, all for the purpose of fun and recording some awesome footage.
